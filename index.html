What is Temp Mail?

Temp mail, also known as temporary email, provides you with disposable email addresses. Use these addresses to avoid spam, protect your privacy, and maintain a clean inbox.

Benefits of Temp Mail

  • Keep spam away from your personal inbox.
  • Maintain your privacy while signing up for services.
  • Use and discard temporary emails without worry.

How to Use Temp Mail?

Simply visit a temp mail, generate an email, and use it for your needs. Once done, discard it!
